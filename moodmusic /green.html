Chill


Green is a color with a rich history. It is also a primary color, which
makes up almost every other color in existance. Often associated with
toxicity and poison but also with money and the environment. Despite
the possible not calm associations with green, when the eye falls on
green one feels calm, relaxed and soothed. This playlist is intended to
reflect that first immediate reaction to green.

Hype


Orange is a vibrant and lively color. The color is associated with a
multitude of emotions and connotations such as citrus, warmth,
energy, excitement, and sourness. When the eye falls on the color,
these emotions resonate and cause a mood within the viewer
similar to its associations that is striking, energizing, and alive.
This playlist is intended to relfect orange in all of its associations
and emotions.

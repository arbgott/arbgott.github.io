Nostalgic


Pink is a soft and airy color. The color is associated with a multitude of
emotions and connotations including femininity, bubble gum, youth,
and joy. When the eye falls on the color, these emotions resonate and
cause a mood within the viewer similar to its associations that is
soothing reminicant, and enthusiatic. This playlist is intended to
relfect pink in all of its associations and emotions.

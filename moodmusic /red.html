Bold


Red is a bold and striking color. It is also a primary color, it makes up
almost every other color in existance. The color is associated with a
multitude of emotions and connotations, including anger, passion,
desire, mobility, and action. When the eye falls on the color, these
emotions resonate and cause a mood within the viewer similar to its
associations. This playlist is intended to relfect red in all of its
associations.

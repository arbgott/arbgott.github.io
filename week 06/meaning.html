Music is Meaning

and Meaning is Cyclical


Music has the power that it does because of personal meaning conflated with the content based on past experience and social living. These factors of musical significance are also the ones that inspire musical creation. You cannot have one without the other. This feeds into the base concept that is widely known: meaning is the foundation for all media. And meaning is a two way street of what you feed into it and what you take from it.
